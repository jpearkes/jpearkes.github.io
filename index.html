About Me

I am a PhD candidate at Stanford University / SLAC studying experimental particle physics. I work on the ATLAS experiment at the Large Hadron Collider.  

My PhD research has focused on HH searches with ATLAS. If you are interested in learning a little bit more about the projects that I have worked on, you can either scroll down, or take a look at my CV.  

If you are wondering how to pronounce my name, it is "Yannika" and comes from Norway.
